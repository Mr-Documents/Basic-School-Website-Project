Academic Programs

Our comprehensive curriculum covers all essential subjects and prepares students for higher education and future careers.

  • Mathematics: Building strong foundations in numbers, algebra, and geometry.
  • Science: Exploring biology, chemistry, and physics through hands-on experiments.
  • English: Developing reading, writing, and communication skills.
  • Social Studies: Understanding history, geography, and civic responsibilities.
  • Arts: Fostering creativity through visual arts, music, and drama.
  • Physical Education: Promoting health and teamwork through sports and activities.
