Academic Programs

Our comprehensive curriculum covers all essential subjects and prepares students for higher education and future careers.

Mathematics

Building strong foundations in numbers, algebra, and geometry. We develop problem-solving skills and critical thinking through interactive learning.

Science

Exploring biology, chemistry, and physics through hands-on experiments. Students discover the wonders of the natural world.

English

Developing reading, writing, and communication skills. We emphasize literature, grammar, and effective expression.

R.M.E (Religious & Moral Education)

Understanding moral values, ethics, and responsibilities. Building character and instilling good values in our students.

Creative Arts

Fostering creativity through visual arts, music, and drama. Students explore their artistic talents and express themselves freely.

French Language

Promoting the act of speaking French for communication opportunities. Students gain proficiency in a global language.

ICT (Information & Communication Technology)

Equipping students with essential digital literacy and computing skills to navigate and succeed in a modern, technology-driven world.

History

Exploring local and global history to understand our heritage, build a sense of identity, and shape a meaningful future for our students.

Mfantse

Promoting our local language and culture through reading, writing, and communication to preserve our rich heritage and traditions.

Committed to providing quality education from nursery to primary levels in a safe and supportive environment.
