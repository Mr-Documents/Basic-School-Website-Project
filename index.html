Welcome to Nana Kotaa School Complex,

Where learning, discipline and excellence are our top priorities. We are committed to provide quality education in a safe, friendly and supportive environment, developing students academically, morally and socially. Our goal is to nurture successful future leaders. We warmly welcome students, parents and visitors to our school community.

A Tradition of Excellence

At Nana Kotaa, we believe that education is the cornerstone of a successful life. Our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to create an atmosphere where students are encouraged to ask questions, explore their interests, and develop a lifelong love for learning.

Our Vision & Academic Pathways

Excellence, discipline and success for all. We offer a structured educational journey designed to support children at every stage of their development.

Nursery & Kindergarten

A warm, play-based environment where our youngest learners develop social skills and early literacy foundations.

Primary School

Focusing on core academic excellence in Mathematics, Science, and Languages while fostering creative thinking.

Junior High (JHS)

Preparing students for the next level with a rigorous curriculum, leadership training, and career guidance.
