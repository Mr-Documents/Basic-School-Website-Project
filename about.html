About Nana Kotaa School Complex

Our School was established to provide quality education and help children develop good moral discipline and knowledge. It started under bamboo buildings and used palm fronds as roofing. The school was established on the 15th on september, 2015. It started with one child and at the end of the term, they were eight.

Our Mission is to educate and inspire learners for a brighter future.

Our Vision

Excellence, discipline and success for all.
